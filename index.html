EU Support for Promoting Sustainable Urban Mobility in Turkish Cities
The overall objective of SUMP Türkiye is to promote individual transport to sustainable, accessible, and inclusive modes of sustainable urban mobility in Turkish cities. To this end, the project aims to support the communication and permanent policy dialogue on sustainable urban mobility among Turkish and EU stakeholders; to build capacities among planners and decision-makers for strategic and integrated sustainable mobility policy solutions; and to enhance the visibility of sustainable urban mobility.
